Electrical Safety Tips

Ensure the safety of your electrical systems with these important tips:

  • Always switch off electrical appliances when not in use.
  • Do not overload electrical sockets and circuits.
  • Regularly inspect and maintain electrical equipment.
  • Hire a qualified technician for electrical installations and repairs.
  • Ensure proper grounding of electrical systems to avoid shock hazards.
